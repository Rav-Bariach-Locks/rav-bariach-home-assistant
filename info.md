# 🔒 Rav Bariach Locks

Control your **Smart Locks** directly from Home Assistant. This integration provides a seamless connection to secure your home and automate your daily routines.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🚪 **Smart Locking:** Lock and unlock your Rav Bariach doors with real-time status updates.
* ⚡ **Service Support:** Trigger specific features and automations directly via Home Assistant services.
* 🧩 **UI Configuration:** Easy setup through the Config Flow interface (No YAML required!).

---

## 🛠️ Requirements

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

* 👤 An active **Rav Bariach** account.
* 🌐 Your Rav Bariach devices must be online and connected to the internet.

---

## ⚙️ How to Setup

1. After downloading this integration via HACS, 🔄 **restart** Home Assistant.
2. Navigate to ⚙️ **Settings** > **Devices & Services**.
3. Click **+ Add Integration** in the bottom right corner.
4. Search for **"Rav Bariach"**.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to log in and authorize your devices.
